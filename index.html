Pairs

Game Explanation

The game Pairs is an appealing alternative to a traditional memmory game for adults. The pictures used show animals. Practizing Pairs on a regular basis could be a nice brain exercise. You can improve your score or time. In short you can look at different images and afterwards you have to find out where the wanted tile is.

Game Rules

After reading the rules carefully push the button "Go to game" below this text. From there you will find yourself at the game page.You have to try to remember the position of the shown animals. Therefore you can either take your time or with the help of the counting timer at the bottom decide on a specif time before you click on the "Flip cards!" button. Then the animal tiles are turned and the comparison tile is revealed. As soon as you click on a turned tile your it will turn and you can see whether it fits or not. If it doesn't you can try again. Your score will be counted on the bottom of the page. If you want to try it again push the bottom "New game" at the head of the page. If you want to read the game rules again, push the button "Rules" at the end of the page and it will direct you back here. Now have fun!
